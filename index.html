Let's dive right in to look At Technology!!

What is Technology?

Technology is the way we apply scientific knowledge for practical aims of human life. It is the set of knowledge, skills, experience and techniques through which humans change, transform and use our environment in order to create tools, machines, products and services that meet our needs and desires.

Merits Of Technology

Technological advancements have accelerated in recent centuries and are more pronounced than ever in our 21st century. It seems that every time there is technological progress in an area, it becomes old news very quickly. Let’s dive right in to look at some advantages technology has brought us.

The improvement of productivity

Technology has played a key role in driving productivity for businesses in a way that would have been unthinkable just a few decades ago.


Better and easier communication between people

Both blue-collar jobs and white-collar jobs have become more efficient due to advancements in technology.

Cheaper Manufacturing of Products

Arguably, the greatest advancement in technology in the last century was the invention of the Internet; it has truly changed every aspect of life and made the world more interconnected.

Demerits Of Technology

Regardless of how far technological advancement and convenient digital technology can be, this level of success comes with many disadvantages. Let’s dive right in to look at some disadvantages technology has brought us.

It can be bad for Environment

Advancements in technology have been overwhelmingly bad for the environment; technology has contributed to environmental deterioration far more quickly than it has contributed to solving environmental problems.


CyberBulling

The Internet has given new meaning to this word — a mob can now be a bunch of anonymous people intent on punishing anyone on social media. It is now easier for people to post hate messages against someone while being completely untraceable. This has given rise to cyberbullying, and it is a problem that governments are finding hard to solve.

Can distract students from studying

Many young students have access to handphones today, especially those living in advanced countries. The problem with mobile devices is that it is a gateway to a world of distractions, from social media posts to memes to YouTube videos.
